setDate

Small 1kb jQuery plugin to display formatted dates.

Download

How to use:

Call the plugin on the set of elements to show the formated date.

Options:

$(selector).setDate(options);

$('elem').setDate({
     date: String||Array||Miliseconds||DateObject,
     format: string,
     months: array,
     days: array
});
Sets the date in the set of elements.
$.formatDate(options);
Returns the formated date. Options are the same as above.


Format Parameters:

All parameters must be preceded by a plus sign to avoid confusion with their respective chars.

This parameters are:

+Y: A full numeric representation of a year, 4 digits.
+y: A two digit representation of a year.
+F: A full textual representation of a month.
+M: A short textual representation of a month, three letters.
+m: Numeric representation of a month, with leading zeros.
+n: Numeric representation of a month, without leading zeros.
+l: (Lowercase 'L') A full textual representation of the day of the week.
+D: A textual representation of a day, three letters.
+d: Day of the month, 2 digits with leading zeros.
+j: Day of the month without leading zeros.
+G: 24-hour format of an hour without leading zeros.
+g: 12-hour format of an hour without leading zeros.
+H: 24-hour format of an hour with leading zeros.
+h: 12-hour format of an hour with leading zeros.
+i: Minutes without leading zeros.
+ii: Minutes with leading zeros.
+s: Seconds without leading zeros.
+ss: Seconds with leading zeros.
+a: Lowercase 'am' or 'pm'.
+A: Uppercase 'AM' or 'PM'.


Examples:


Default:
$('.setDate1').setDate();
Date string:
$('.setDate2').setDate({date: "October 23, 1975 11:13:00"});
Date array:
$('.setDate3').setDate({date: [79,5,24]});
Miliseconds:
$('.setDate4').setDate({date: 1371146201884});
Date object:
var date = new Date();
$('.setDate5').setDate({date: date});
Custom format:
$('.setDate6').setDate({format: "Today is: +l +j of +F of +Y. And the time is: +G:+ii +a."});
Custom language:
$('.setDate7').setDate({
     format: "Hoy es: +l +j de +F de +Y. Y la hora es: +H:+ii +a.",
     months: ["Enero","Febrero","Marzo","Abril","Mayo","Junio","Julio","Agosto","Septiembre","Octubre","Noviembre","Diciembre"],
     days: ["Domingo","Lunes","Martes","Miércoles","Jueves","Viernes","Sábado"]
});
